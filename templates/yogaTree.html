INSTRUCTIONS:

1. Begin in a standing position with your feet hip-distance apart and your arms by your sides.

2. Shift your weight onto your left foot and lift your right foot, placing the sole of your foot against your left thigh or calf.

3. Press your foot and thigh/calf together, engaging your standing leg and glutes for balance.

4. Bring your hands together at your heart center or reach them overhead, with your palms facing each other.

5. Hold the pose for several breaths, then release and repeat on the other side.
