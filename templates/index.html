Welcome to FitHub!

Welcome to our exercise and yoga web app! We are thrilled to provide you with a platform that combines the benefits of exercise and yoga to promote physical and mental well-being. Our app is designed to help you achieve your fitness goals by providing you with personalized routines and real-time feedback on your form and alignment. We understand that finding the time and resources to attend a fitness class or hire a personal trainer can be challenging. That's why we created this app - to bring the convenience and accessibility of fitness and yoga to your fingertips. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced practitioner, our app has something for everyone. With a range of pre-recorded exercise and yoga classes, customizable routines, and real-time pose detection technology, our app is designed to cater to your unique needs and preferences. You can track your progress, set goals, and receive reminders to stay on track with your fitness journey. We believe that fitness and yoga are not just about physical exercise, but also about mental and emotional well-being. That's why our app provides you with a holistic approach to wellness that focuses on improving your overall quality of life. We hope you enjoy using our exercise and yoga web app and that it helps you achieve your fitness goals. Let's start the journey towards a healthier and happier you!
